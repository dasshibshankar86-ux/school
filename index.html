Welcome to School

Experience Our Campus: Inspiring Minds and Shaping Futures

Kushbasan High School has to its credit seven decades and more of an enriching journey during which progress and achievements have reached incredible summits on the strength of an exceptional vision and mission, as well as an untiring effort to incorporate the new, without bypassing the old. In all these years, despite all the sweeping changes, the school remains rooted in its commitment to give the best education, infuse values into the students and build a better future.

Over the years, the school which welcomed twenty children on a distant summer day has spread its rainbow wings in much the same way as its students have. The journey has been a magical one, marked by splendid milestones. The comely mud built houses soon grew into newer, bigger buildings that could bring more and more children into its fold. The early faculty handed the batons over to a burgeoning number of teachers who were equally committed and fully trained to keep up with the changing times.

Pedagogy / Curriculum


Our curriculum is designed in such a manner that students learn through hands on experience (experientiallearning) with the help of 5A's. High quality teacher's training is provided by XSEED (A Singapore based company) for Online classes (live-interactive) and offline classes.

XSEED is an academic solution designed to raise the level of learning in classrooms and build confidence in children. XSEED follows the proven 5- step experiential learning method where concepts are taught through hands on experimentation followed by questioning, application and feedback. This replaces the one step 'telling' and memorisation followed in many traditional classrooms. With XSEED, parents see that their children are confident and ready with skills required for higher studies and career.
